Bridge Bet

Take the bet and build the bridge.

Multi Billionire, Jon Rich, comes to you, Ron Luis a world renowned bridge architect. He bets that you can't build a bridge out of object found in the woods. You are out to prove him WRONG. For "good" luck he gives you his lucky monocle. Unbeknowst to you, it is really a tracking device. When you get to the woods, Jon Rich sends out bullies to your location to keep you from collecting needed items.

Computer controls: Use arrow keys to guide the Ron Luis to collect and build the bridge. Jump by using space on enemies to destroy them.

Ouya Controls: Move using the left joystick and jump by using O. Destroy enemies by jumping on them. To place the bridge tiles A, Y, U

Platforms available for:

  • Mac
  • Windows
  • Ouya
